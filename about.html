Discover the story behind the lens

I’m Louison Martin, a passionate photographer based in Lyon, France. My journey began in the summer of 2024, driven by curiosity and a deep desire to capture the beauty of the world around me. From serene natural landscapes to vibrant urban scenes, I approach each photo instinctively, allowing the moment to guide my lens. Equipped with my Canon EOS 550D and a Sony A7 III, along with access to a studio and a Parrot Anafi drone, I strive to create timeless visuals that reflect the beauty and emotion of every scene.

Whether it’s through spontaneous captures or carefully composed shots, my goal is to tell stories that resonate and evoke emotions, immortalizing moments that matter.

My Gear

Canon EOS 550D

The Canon EOS 550D is a digital SLR camera with an 18-megapixel APS-C sensor. It is ideal for landscape photography, portraits, and any type of image requiring superior image quality.

Parrot Anafi Drone

The Parrot Anafi is a compact drone with a 4K HDR camera, perfect for capturing stunning aerial shots. It offers remarkable flight stability and great flexibility for photography and video.

Sony A7 III

The Sony A7 III is a full-frame camera with excellent low-light performance and fast autofocus. Although I cannot use it all the time, it is perfect for capturing high-quality shots in various environments.
